The **Conduit PDX-2** transforms your modular system into a mobile powerhouse. Power an entire case from a single USB-C laptop charger-delivering massive, studio-grade current with zero weight penalty and zero setup friction.

At just 15mm deep and 4HP wide, it provides enough current (3.0A on +12V, 1.5A on -12V) to drive 15 to 20 modules. It’s the end of heavy wall-warts and bulky external power bricks.

## Real-Time Power Monitoring

The "Reactor Core" display gives you instant visibility into your case's load. Dual 5-segment bar graphs monitor +12V and -12V rails independently, allowing you to balance your system and catch power-hungry modules or transient spikes before they become issues.

The interface is designed for immediate, at-a-glance diagnostics-no meters or external tools required.

## Studio-Grade Performance

Portability doesn't mean compromising on signal integrity. The PDX-2 is engineered for the lowest possible noise floor, ensuring your oscillators stay in tune and your high-gain stages stay silent.

- **High-Frequency Architecture:** Operates at 2.2 MHz to eliminate audible switching whine.
- **Advanced Filtration:** Multi-stage pi-filters keep voltage ripple below 5mVpp.
- **Clean Negative Rail:** Dedicated inverting topology ensures a silent -12V foundation for analog modules.

## Industrial-Strength Safety

The PDX-2 handles the engineering so you can focus on the music. It includes a full suite of hardware protections to keep your modules safe under any conditions:

- **Sequencing Interlock:** Ensures the -12V rail only starts once the +12V rail is stable.
- **Short-Circuit & Thermal Protection:** Instantaneous shutdown during faults.
- **Reverse Polarity Clamps:** Protects against power bus wiring errors.
- **TVS Suppression:** Guards against voltage spikes from external power sources.

## Power From Anywhere

Ditch the search for specific power adapters. Bring your rack wherever there is USB-C:
- **Travel:** Power your rack from a standard 65W laptop charger.
- **Field:** Run your system for hours from a USB-C battery pack (20V support required).
- **Studio:** Simplify your setup with a single cable for power and data.

The PDX-2 automatically negotiates the 20V Power Delivery profile, providing high-efficiency conversion with over 92% efficiency.
