## II. The Repository Strategy (The Log / `pageType: repository`)

The template is named **Repository**, and the content structure is optimized for narrative technical writing (**The Log**).

### A. Component Strategy & Naming

*   **`<CodeBlock />`**: Used for all code. Labels: `// LOG ENTRY: CODE`
*   **`<SchematicEmbed />`**: Used for all hardware images.

### B. Content Title Examples (Log-Focused)

The titles emphasize the narrative of an engineering log:

1.  **Log Entry #42: The Unexpected SvelteKit Endpoints That Made My Hardware Backend Hum.**
2.  **Deep Dive: Building a Generative Visualizer with P5.js and Discovering My Code's Latency Problem.**
3.  **Adventures in Synthesis: Why My Clean Code Still Needs A Filter Capacitor (A Log of Debugging My Eurorack).**
