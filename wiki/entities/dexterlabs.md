# Entity: Dexterlabs

Dexterlabs is the personal brand and company of Dexter, focusing on the intersection of hardware hacking, generative art, and software engineering.

## Key Domains
- **Eurorack Hardware**: Modular synthesizer components (PDX-2, AR-1).
- **Generative Art**: Exploring algorithmic visual systems.
- **Agentic Workflows**: Implementing patterns like the LLM Wiki for efficient engineering.

## Brand Values
- **Technical Credibility**: Honest documentation and studio-grade performance.
- **Minimalism**: Focus on functional clarity (Transitioning to "Lab Bench" aesthetic).
- **Transparency**: Open-sourcing processes and thoughts.
