# Dexterlabs

> Sources: Internal, 2026-04-26
> Updated: 2026-04-26

## Overview

Dexterlabs is Dexter's side venture — a vehicle to give hobby projects structure, visibility, and a modest income stream. Not a startup. A working engineer selling things he actually built.

Dexter is a software and hardware engineer: 15+ years on Linux, fluent in TypeScript and Python, at home in the full web stack. Also builds hardware — Eurorack modules, Arduino-based projects, 3D-printed enclosures. The site reflects both sides.

## What it sells

- Eurorack synthesizer modules (e.g. AR-1) — live in the `distrans` repo
- Cables and accessories
- Potentially: custom development work for others

## What the site does

The website is the storefront, devlog, and portfolio in one. It's not a marketing site — it's a working engineer's public bench. Products are listed, but so is the process: failures, experiments, and half-finished ideas.

## Brand values

- **Honest** — document the process, not a polished narrative
- **Minimal** — nothing on the page that doesn't earn its place
- **Technical** — the audience is people who understand what they're looking at
