# Workflow: Lint Wiki

Health check for the knowledge base.

## Execution Steps

1. **Link Verification**: Find and fix broken internal links.
2. **Orphan Search**: Identify pages not linked from the index.
3. **Logic Check**: Find contradictions between old log entries and current entity pages.
4. **Gap Analysis**: Detect missing documentation for mentioned concepts.
5. **Report**: Present findings to the USER.
